**The Financial Architecture of Power: Decoding India’s Battery Energy Storage (BESS) Revolution**

As India advances toward its **500 GW non‑fossil fuel target by 2030**, the focus is shifting from power generation to power management. Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) have emerged as the critical enabler of grid stability and renewable integration.

This document breaks down the BESS landscape in 2026 across financial, technical, and regulatory dimensions.
